KidSchedule helps parents build calm, predictable days for their children with the help of AI.

Tell KidSchedule about your child — age, interests, school timing, and what you want to focus on this week — and get a ready-to-use daily routine in seconds. Adjust it with a tap, share it with your co-parent, and let your kid earn reward points for finishing their tasks.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Generate a personalized daily routine for each child using AI
• Switch between Today and Tomorrow views without losing context
• Track task completion and award points your kid will love
• Build life-skill habits — reading, screen-time limits, chores, bedtime
• Follow gentle guidance for tricky moments: tantrums, picky eating, sleep
• Manage routines for multiple children from one parent dashboard

WHY PARENTS LOVE IT
• No more "what's next?" — kids see a clear plan they can follow
• Less negotiation, more cooperation, thanks to the reward system
• AI does the planning so you don't have to start from scratch every week
• Works for toddlers all the way to pre-teens

PRIVACY & SAFETY
KidSchedule is built for families. We never show ads to children, and routines stay private to your account. See our privacy policy inside the app for details.
